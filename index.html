Jamie Yeats is a Michigan representative for ShelterBox, an international disaster relief charity that delivers emergency shelter, warmth and dignity to people affected by disaster worldwide, and is available for ShelterBox demonstrations / presentations at your meetings and events.

Jamie volunteers at the International Institute of Metropolitan Detroit where he assists people to become U.S. citizens. He is also the founder and principal of Y8S, a systems engineering and program management consultancy.

Concerned about maintaining his geek quotient Jamie is interested in applications of physical and mathematical models to public policy issues, open source software, and complexity. He writes and rants at the Null Hypothesis and Heteroscadasticity and tweets occasionally as @jaime27.

Most at home on (and in) the Holy Waters of the Au Sable river in northern Michigan his other interests include orienteering, fly fishing, building and using wooden boats, cross country skiing, and Sacred Harp singing.
